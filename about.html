Hello, my name is Steven and I'm a programmer. I originally started out as a biochemist, however, I found out that it really wasn't for me. I've always been interested in computers and programming growing up so after much debate, I decided to give it a try by enrolling into a bootcamp. It was really fun experience and I thoroughly enjoyed it. Afterwards, I had no idea what I wanted to pursue in this broad new field, so I started freelancing to test the waters. Freelancing has been pretty fun, there's always something unique that a client needs and the thrill of figuring out how to do something is always exciting.

I am always looking out for my next adventure, so feel free to browse the projects section to check out what I've built, reach out or connect on linkedIn.
